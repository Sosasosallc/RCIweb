About Royal Christian Institute

The Royal Christian Institute is the brainchild and a product of the creative and humanitarian efforts of Ms. Rose T. Songor, a Liberian-American living in Indianapolis, Indiana (USA). Located In Indianapolis, Indiana, RCI supports our youth (in collaboration with local churches and other entities) to help them remain focused on developmental and progressive activities when they are out of school. Currently, RCI's most urgent need (in Indianapolis) is to expand feeding program(s) and to establish a daycare center for children. In order to underwrite overall operational costs we need to generate funds.

Ms. SONGOR VISITED WAR-TORN LIBERIA IN 2010 and saw first-hand the desperate need for educating the children of Monrovia. The three civil wars in Sierra Leone and Liberia left a legacy of poverty and social upheaval, especially for young citizens whose development was interrupted by conflict. With her own money she bought a piece of land on which she intends to build "The Royal Christian Institute." She has already initiated the construction of a kindergarten through 12th grade school (The architectural design is ready) in the much challenged Trye community of Bardnersville, Monrovia. Ms. Songor or "Tewa" as she is fondly referred to by those close to her is a generous human being who loves children and spends hours and hours on volunteer work helping them.

Our Mission

The Royal Christian Institute (RCI) is dedicated to empowering youth through educational and developmental programs. We aim to support youth in Indianapolis, expand feeding programs, establish a daycare center, and build a school in Liberia. Inspired by the vision and humanitarian efforts of Ms. Rose T. Songor, RCI is driven by a commitment to transforming lives through education and community support. Our primary focus is on children and youth in Indianapolis and Liberia.To achieve our goals, we will collaborate with local entities, secure funding, hire qualified staff, complete construction projects, and launch fundraising campaigns. Guided by Ms. Songor's dedication and generosity, we strive to make a lasting impact on the lives of children.

Our Vision:

Our vision is to ensure that every child has a stable access to education, basic human needs met and a hopeful path ahead. Our mission is to support our youth through action and advocacy. RCI is a social advocacy organization committed to solving child education.
