Royale Christian Institute

Welcome! We are a non-profit dedicated to providing housing and education opportunities for children in need. Our mission is to ensure every child has a safe place to call home and access to quality education. Through our programs and partnerships, we strive to create a brighter future for children and break the cycle of poverty.
